Life in Bloom - Site Plan

1. Site Name

Life in Bloom

This site name reflects my personal passion for gardening. I grew up on a farm with a large garden and have a Bachelor's degree in agriculture. Gardening is part of who I am, and I want to share its benefits with others.

2. Site Purpose

The website will promote the benefits of home gardening, share profiles of common garden plants, and allow visitors to suggest plants or gardening tips.

3. Target Audience Scenarios

  • Janette wants to start a garden in her apartment and visits the site for beginner-friendly plant ideas.
  • Jerid is an experienced gardener who uses the suggestion form to share tips and recommend plants.

4. Color Scheme

Lavender (#b497bd): Used for headings and accents.

Soft Light Green (#7b9e87): Used for main headings and highlight sections.

Warm Beige (#d8c3a5): Used for backgrounds, borders, and subtle details.

5. Typography

Roboto will be used for both headings and body text. .
